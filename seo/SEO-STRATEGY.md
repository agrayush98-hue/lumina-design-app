# Lumina Design — SEO Strategy
**Business:** Lumina Design (lightillumina.com)
**Type:** B2B SaaS — Lighting Design Software
**Market:** India (primary), Global (secondary)
**Date:** May 2026

---

## Business Context

Lumina Design is a cloud-based lighting design platform targeting architects, electrical consultants, and lighting engineers in India. The product competes with desktop-first tools (DIALux, Relux, AGi32) that are expensive, Windows-only, and have no Indian pricing. The primary moat is web-based access + Indian pricing (₹0–₹2,949/month) + DALI 2.0 automation.

**Core advantage for SEO:** The main competitors are legacy desktop tools with zero content marketing. The space is wide open for a modern SaaS to own all the informational and commercial keywords.

## E-E-A-T Building Plan

Currently Lumina Design has **zero E-E-A-T signals**. This must be fixed.

1. **Experience:** Add a founder story page. Who built this? Why? Real people, real backstory.
2. **Expertise:** Author bios on all blog posts with credentials (lighting engineer, IES member, etc.)
3. **Authoritativeness:** Get listed on:
   - IndiaMART / Capterra / G2 / Software Suggest (free listings)
   - IES India (Illuminating Engineering Society)
   - BIS/NBC India standards references in content
4. **Trustworthiness:**
   - Add real customer testimonials with names + company names
   - Add GST number / CIN on about/contact page
   - Show real uptime stats (not just "99.9%")

---

## GEO / AI Search Readiness

- Add `llms.txt` (15 minutes, immediate impact for AI crawlers)
- Add `SoftwareApplication` schema with complete feature list
- Structure all content with clear H2/H3 hierarchy — AI systems extract these
- Add FAQ sections to feature pages and pricing page
- Publish original data: "Lux requirements for Indian office spaces per NBC 2016"
- Get cited in industry content by writing for architecture/MEP publications
